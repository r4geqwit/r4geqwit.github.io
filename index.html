About

Data Scientist and IT professional with experience using Python, SQL, and PowerBi. Experience and trained in Project Management, Data Modeling, and Data Analysis. Hello my name is Alejandro and I have many years of experience working in the field of IT. My experience ranges from your basic break/fix support for hardware and software, and up to network troubleshooting. I also have worked on many complex projects, including office expansion, hardware refreshes, and onboarding of new staff. But all of that is kind of bland in my opinion, likely because I have done this for "many years." I really want to work in the field of artificial intelligence and machine learning. I've always been intrigued at how devices can be given instructions and know what to do, or given partial facts and provide a credible "guess" as to what the outcome may be. Growing up I never knew what that was or how to follow that kind of path. I knew it had something to do with computers, so I decided to train in that field.. Well many years later and things did not go as planned. Here I am though with reinvigorated hope to finally pursue this career. Being comfortable with change is something I have always been comfortable with, and my best approach usually has been to just go for it. Connect with me if you'd like to add value to your network with someone that possesses "many" years of experience in IT, not as many years experience in Data Science, and someone who likes playing sports. Fun fact: My latest favorite piece of technology has to be something very simple. Trello. Such a simple tool, but with enough creativity, it can be very useful in every day life.
